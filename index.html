I’m Sorry Babu ❤️

Babu… I know I messed up. Not just by getting insecure, but by how I reacted and spoke to you.

You were just being yourself, enjoying with your friends, and instead of trusting you, I questioned you. That wasn’t fair, darling.

And what I said about my past… comparing you… I regret that deeply. You are special because you are YOU.

The way you tease me, the way you flirt with me… I didn’t realize it before, but I’m actually falling for you.

You’re changing me, darling… I was someone with a lot of ego, someone who never said sorry easily…

But today, I am here, because losing you hurts more than my ego ever mattered.

I understand now — love isn’t control. It’s trust, space, and respect.

I don’t want to cage you, babu. I want to be someone who supports you and trusts you.

I’m not asking you to forget everything instantly… just one chance to prove myself.

Will you forgive me? ❤️
